Jonathan Rhys Meyers Biography and Wiki

He has also appeared on several notable tv shows such as Elvis, The Tudors, Dracula and Vikings. He has won several Awards such as Golden Globe Award, and nominations such as Golden Globe Award nominations and Primetime Emmy Award nomination.

Jonathan Rhys Meyers Vikings

Meyers appeared in the Vikings television series as a starring character portraying Bishop Heahmund. Vikings is an Irish historical drama or as well action-adventure television series that was created and written by Michael Hirst.

The series was filmed in Ireland and was released on 3rd March 2013 in Canada. It has been running until its fifth season was ordered to be its final season in 2017. The sixth season of the series was titled Vikings: Valhalla and it premiered on 4th December 2019 and was aired in Netflix.

Jonathan Starred in season 4 to season 6. The creator and the writer of the series explained his role as a ‘warrior Bishop’ and he said “I was looking at the history books, and I came across these warrior bishops.

The antecedents of the Knights Templar: these are people who were absolutely religious, yet they put on armor and they fought. Don’t let their priestly status fool you, either.

‘They were crazy! They believed totally in Christianity and the message, and yet, on the battlefield, they were totally berserk.’ This is the role that he portrayed through his cast in Vikings.
