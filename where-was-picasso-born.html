Where did Picasso grow up?

Pablo Ruiz was born in Malaga on 25 October 1881, the son of an art teacher. He later adopted his mother’s maiden name of Picasso. He grew up in Barcelona, showing artistic talent at an early age. In the early 1900s, he moved between France and Spain before finally settling in Paris in 1904.

Why did MF Hussain paint horses?

When Hussain painted the horses for the first time in the 1950s, it was certain that he sought much more in the piece than a mere aesthetic exercise. A personal meditation plays out on the canvas and Husain’s desire to universalise a personal and intimate relationship with the animal is evident.
