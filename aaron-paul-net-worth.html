The American actor appeared in all five seasons of “Breaking Bad,” which aired from January 2008 to September 2013.

Aaron Paul | Career

As a child, Aaron began participating in church plays. He enrolled in his school’s theatre department during eighth grade and later decided to act his profession and move to LA, California.

Nonetheless, pursuing an acting career wasn’t easy, and he had to struggle for a decade. Later, he got into modeling and eventually started doing commercials.

For the initial few years, Paul did minor roles in TV shows like ‘Melrose Place,’ ‘The X-Files,’ ‘Judging Amy,’ ‘CSI: Miami,’ and ‘Point Pleasant.’

Depicting Scott Quittman in the HBO drama series ‘Big Love’ which aired from 2007 to 2011, he garnered a sizable fanbase.

Anyway, his absolute breakthrough was AMC hit series “Breaking Bad,” that aired from 2008 to 2013.

In 2019, Paul returned as Jesse Pinkman in the spin-off movie El Camino: A Breaking Bad.
