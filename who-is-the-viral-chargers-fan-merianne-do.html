Who is The Viral Chargers Fan, Merianne Do?

 Posted on May 26, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  535 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Merianne Do is a football fan who has gone viral the past few days, with a lot of people suspecting she is not a real football fan but something else. As her name continues to make headlines online, people have been searching for more information about her.

As your go-to website, we have scavenged the internet space in an attempt to bring you all we know about her and whether or not people’s suspicions of her not being a real fan are legitimate or not. Kindly follow this article for all the details about Merianne Do.

The fan, Merianne Do, gained notoriety for showing intense emotions following each play at a game recently.

When the Los Angeles Chargers lost to the Dallas Cowboys on Monday Night Football, Merianne Do, one of the Los Angeles Chargers fans, went viral, appearing to feel every emotion. As she went through a Greek tragedy, the cameras kept panning to her, leading some online to believe she was an actor.

After witnessing the fan’s life and death during each play of the 20-17 defeat, social media users began to doubt her sincerity.

Los Angeles Chargers Confirm Merianne Do Is Real

As the suspicions on social media intensify about Merianne Do not being real, the Los Angeles Chargers have come out to confirm that she is very real.

Together with her spouse, Tuan Pham, Merianne Do holds season tickets for the Chargers. Do contacted the Chargers in an effort to convince them to tell the world that her love for the team is as genuine as it gets after finding that some people thought she was a fake.

Tom Pelissero wrote on X, saying, “Sorry, conspiracy theorists: The #Chargers fan who went viral Monday night and her husband are season ticket holders who rent cabana suites every season for multiple games, I’m told.”

“They’re true diehard fans — not actors — and wanted to get the word out.”

Merianne Do’s night came to an end in sadness as Justin Herbert’s interception stopped the Chargers’ potential game-winning drive. Los Angeles, now 2-3 and third in the AFC West, suffered another discouraging defeat.

Week 8’s clash between the Los Angeles Chargers and the Kansas City Chiefs is not going to be much easier for the Chargers, and since the game will be held in Arrowhead Stadium, Do will have to watch it from her couch while feeling a range of emotions.

Who Is Merianne Do?

Merianne Do is an NFL fan who is said to have Chargers season tickets with her husband, Tuan Pham. She went viral for her intense emotions at a Los Angeles Chargers game against the Cowboys.

Do is a longtime California resident who has worked as a pharmacist for Walgreens for more than 20 years, according to a report on X from Leighton Glodek. She had access to suite-level seats at SoFi Stadium, where the cameras were pointed directly at her, because her husband worked as the CMO for CoreVest Finance.

This information was confirmed by NFL Network insider Tom Pelissero, who wrote on X that she and her husband rent cabana suites for many games each season as season ticket holders. Pelissero did not formally identify the fan, though.
